Alternative Currencies

Gold and crypto are two noteworthy alternatives to sovereign currencies. Gold has long been a reserve asset while crypto has yet to become a serious option. Both are widely traded in the market, but are rarely used for global payments. The landscape may change for crypto when it gains acceptance with time.
